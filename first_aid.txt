Topic: Basic First Aid Awareness

Overview:
First aid refers to immediate assistance given to a person suffering from sudden illness or injury before professional medical help arrives.

Common Situations:
- Burns: Cool the affected area under running water
- Bleeding: Apply pressure using a clean cloth
- Fainting: Lay the person down and elevate legs
- Fractures: Immobilize the injured area

Importance:
First aid can prevent a condition from worsening and may save lives during emergencies.

Limitations:
First aid does not replace professional medical treatment.
